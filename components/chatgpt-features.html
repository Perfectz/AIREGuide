Conversation Context

ChatGPT maintains context across messages in a conversation, allowing for follow-up questions and refined responses. In real estate, use this to build on previous property details without repeating information.

  • Tip: Start with a detailed prompt, then refine with 'Expand on the kitchen features.'
  • Best for: Iterative ad copy creation or persona development.

Canvas

An interactive workspace for collaborative editing with AI. Upload documents or start from scratch, and have ChatGPT suggest edits in real-time.

  • Tip: Use for drafting listing descriptions; AI can highlight Fair Housing compliance issues.
  • Best for: Team reviews of marketing materials.

Image Upload

Upload property photos for AI analysis, generating descriptions or improvement suggestions.

  • Tip: Upload a room photo and ask 'Generate a compelling listing description based on this image.'
  • Best for: Creating visual-based content or virtual staging ideas.

Model Type Selection

Choose between models like GPT-4 for complex tasks or GPT-3.5 for quick responses. Premium users access advanced models.

  • Tip: Use GPT-4 for detailed market analysis; GPT-3.5 for simple prompt testing.
  • Best for: Balancing speed and quality in real estate research.

Long-Term Memory

ChatGPT can remember key details across sessions (via custom instructions or saved chats), building a personalized knowledge base.

  • Tip: Set custom instructions with your agency's branding guidelines for consistent outputs.
  • Best for: Maintaining client preferences or market data over time.

Voice Chat

ChatGPT supports voice input and output, enabling hands-free conversations and audio responses for on-the-go use.

  • Tip: Use voice chat for quick property queries while driving between showings.
  • Best for: Brainstorming marketing ideas during commutes or generating quick responses to client questions.
