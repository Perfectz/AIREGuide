The Persona-Driven Multimedia Workflow

This is the culmination of everything we've learned. We're using our Core Formula to create assets that are perfectly aligned with our brand and our target client.

  1. 1
    Step 1: The Voice (ElevenLabs)

    Goal: Turn your written brand voice into an actual voice.

    How it Connects: The [Desired Tone/Style] from our formula becomes the direction for the AI voice actor.

    Example Prompt: "Act as a professional voiceover artist. Using my defined Brand Voice ('warm, professional, and trustworthy'), narrate the following script for a property tour video. The delivery should be calm and reassuring, not a hard sell. Script: 'Welcome to 123 Maple Lane, a home where comfort meets elegance...'"

  2. 2
    Step 2: The Visuals (Midjourney)

    Goal: Create images that reflect your customer's dreams.

    How it Connects: The [Target Audience] from our formula dictates the entire scene. We're not just creating a picture of a house; we're creating a picture of our persona's ideal life.

    Example Prompt: "Act as a creative director for a lifestyle photoshoot. Generate a photorealistic image for a Facebook ad targeting our 'Upsizing Family' persona. The image should show a happy family of four enjoying a summer BBQ in the spacious, green backyard of a suburban home. The style should be candid, warm, and aspirational. Aspect ratio 16:9. --style raw"

  3. 3
    Step 3: The Face (Hedra)

    Goal: Build a personal connection with an avatar.

    How it Connects: This brings your Personal Persona to life, allowing you to deliver a perfectly crafted message every time.

    Example Prompt (for the script): "Act as a friendly real estate agent. Write a 15-second script for a Hedra avatar video that will be used as a personal welcome on a listing page. The tone must match my Brand Voice ('approachable and expert'). Script: 'Hi, I'm [Your Name]. I'm so excited to show you this beautiful home. The feature that I think you'll love most is...'"

  4. 4
    Step 4: Assemble in CapCut

    Goal: Combine all your AI-generated assets into a polished, professional video ad.

    How it Connects: This is the final step where you become the director. Import your ElevenLabs voiceover, your Midjourney images, and your Hedra avatar clip into CapCut. Add text overlays, background music, and your branding to create a cohesive story.
