2 · Structured Prompt Formula & Output

Prompt (Formula Applied)
Act as an award-winning Atlanta real‑estate copywriter and write a 180‑word MLS/Zillow listing description—with an eye‑catching headline and five persuasive bullet highlights—for the freshly updated 2‑bed, 2.5‑bath, 1,520‑sq‑ft brick townhome at 335 Towergate Place, Sandy Springs (Atlanta) GA 30350. Highlight the granite‑and‑stainless chef’s kitchen, private fenced patio, dual en‑suite bedrooms, ultra‑low voluntary HOA (~$75/yr), and the unbeatable location minutes from GA‑400, Perimeter Mall, and Chattahoochee River parks. Use an upbeat, lifestyle‑driven tone that sparks FOMO and creates a sense of urgency for young professionals and small families seeking turnkey convenience, outdoor recreation, and a savvy long‑term investment.

4 · Why the Formula Wins

  1. Role Priming = Authority Boost — “Award‑winning Atlanta copywriter” unlocks insider vocabulary & persuasive flair.
  2. Length & Format Constraints = MLS‑Ready — 180‑word cap forces headline + bullets that MLS/Zillow display perfectly.
  3. Context & Key Details = Relevance — Feeding granular facts (HOA fee, patio, bedroom layout) tells the model what to sell.
  4. Tone + Audience Targeting = Emotional Hook — FOMO language resonates with young pros & small families.
  5. Bullet Highlights = Skimmability — 5 bold bullets convey value in <8 s of eye‑scan time.
  6. Built‑in Compliance Safety — Format limits reduce Fair‑Housing risk by focusing on features, not protected classes.
